Complete expertise for your property

Whether you're preparing your home for sale, moving into a new condo, or simply want to bring a tired interior back to life, our team of painters in Longueuil delivers clean, fast, and durable work. We work in Vieux-Longueuil, Saint-Hubert and Greenfield Park, and we adapt our approach to the type of building.

What's included in our service

  • Interior painting — walls, ceilings, moldings, doors and frames, two coats minimum.
  • Exterior painting — wood siding, fiber cement, soffits, fascias, balconies.
  • Surface preparation — sanding, caulking, filling, the right primer.
  • Drywall taping & plastering — crack repair, drywall, invisible joints.
  • Full protection — furniture, floors, light fixtures, plants.
  • Color guidance — samples applied directly to your walls before the final pick.

Condo painting specialists

New condominiums often hide drywall flaws, joints that show in the first year, and walls that mark with the slightest contact. We fix these defects at the source, choose top-grade washable paints (Benjamin Moore Aura, Sico Cashmere) and coordinate with your condo board to respect quiet hours.

Staircase & floor painting

A Quebec home classic: bringing a wood staircase scarred by years back to life, or coating a garage floor in epoxy. We use slip-resistant, durable, easy-care products. The finish lasts for years, even with kids, pets and winter boots.

Our commitment

Every project comes with a 3-year written warranty on labor. If a drywall joint reappears, a coat peels, or a touch-up is needed, we come back at no charge. That's what you should expect from a professional painter holding an RBQ license.
