A painting company built for facility managers

When you hire a painter for a commercial space, it isn't really about color — it's about logistics. The painter has to get in after hours, leave before opening, leave no smell or trace, and bill exactly what was quoted. That's our standard.

Environments we serve

  • Office towers and corporate floors — Longueuil, Brossard, Boucherville.
  • Medical and dental clinics — low-VOC, antimicrobial paints, code-compliant.
  • Retail and restaurants — fast execution, high-durability washable paints.
  • Boardrooms and prestige lobbies — premium finish, accent walls, decorative treatments.
  • Warehouses and light industrial spaces — epoxy paints, floor markings, laser-straight lines.
  • Co-ops, schools and daycares — certified paints, low odor, fast drying.

Why facility managers call us back

Detailed quote, broken down by zone, with exact paint brands and color codes. Precise schedule, integrated with your operations. Single point of contact, reachable directly by phone. Compliant invoicing with RBQ license number and GST/QST registrations. And above all: no surprise price adjustments at the end of the job.

Compliance and insurance

Our crews carry their ASP construction safety cards, we are registered with the CNESST, we hold $2 million in liability insurance, and our RBQ license covers commercial painting work. All documents are provided on request before the job starts.
