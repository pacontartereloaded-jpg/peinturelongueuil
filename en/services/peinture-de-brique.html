A visible — and lasting — transformation

Brick painting has become the #1 option for modernizing bungalows, cottages and duplexes in Vieux-Longueuil, Saint-Hubert and Greenfield Park. Done right, it lasts 15 to 25 years, raises resale value, and gives an aging building a truly contemporary look.

Our brick method

  • Full mortar inspection and identification of zones to repair before painting.
  • Controlled-pressure washing that doesn't damage the joints.
  • Mortar repair and crack caulking.
  • Mineral primer that penetrates the brick instead of sealing it.
  • Two coats of breathable mineral paint (Keim, Beeck) — the brick keeps releasing moisture.
  • Written warranty on application.

The colors that actually work
