Surface preparation

Drywall finishing in Longueuil — the finish starts inside the wall.

Plastering, sanding, drywall and crack repair. The invisible step that separates decent work from a wall that looks like polished glass under the light.

Free estimate

What we actually do

  • New drywall installation and patching on walls and ceilings.
  • Three-pass joint taping — compound, tape, finish. Sanding between every coat.
  • Plaster crack repair in Vieux-Longueuil's century-old homes.
  • Hole, anchor and bulging-joint repair in new condos.
  • Ceiling smoothing & texture removal (including popcorn ceiling removal).
  • Caulking of moldings, frames and baseboards before painting.
  • Sealer primer matched to the wall — new, old, stained or repaired.

Why it matters

Premium paint applied to a poorly prepared wall is still a poorly prepared wall. Defects show up the moment evening light hits the surface at an angle. A properly taped, properly primed wall, on the other hand, looks flawless under any light — that's the difference your friends will notice without being able to explain it.

The pitfalls of new condos

Condos delivered less than two years ago often reveal flaws after the first heating season: cracking joints, ceiling stains, screws backing out. We rework all those zones, sometimes deeply, so the corrections actually hold.
