Area — Vieux-Longueuil

A local painter, for the neighborhood that deserves more than average.

Century-old homes on rue Saint-Charles, prestige condos at Quai-de-Longueuil, co-ownerships near the cathedral. We understand the quirks of every kind of building in the Old Town.

Free estimateSee local projects

Working with heritage — without disfiguring it

Vieux-Longueuil homes have character: original moldings, real plaster instead of drywall, solid wood staircases, wood windows. The wrong modern paint can destroy that character in two days. The right paint, conversely, brings it out and preserves it.

What we typically handle in the area

  • Restoration of original moldings and woodwork — careful stripping, enamel finish that will last 20 years.
  • Plaster & structural crack repair in century homes.
  • Kitchen and bathroom painting in renovated condos along the river.
  • Sober, noble color palettes — anthracite, warm whites, forest greens that suit the neighborhood.
  • Coordination with condo boards for permitted hours and approved colors.

Why owners in the Old Town call us

Because we know the difference between repainting and reviving. Because we protect century-old hardwood floors as if they were our own. Because we don't show up with a five-painter crew who don't know each other. And because we respect the quiet rhythm of a neighborhood that doesn't need noise to live.

Recent projects

Victorian home near Marie-Victorin park (full interior, 7 days), 1500 sq ft condo with river view (kitchen + living room, 4 days), triplex on rue Saint-Jean (facade and ground floor, 9 days).
