Our services for Saint-Hubert

  • Full interior painting of bungalows and splits — typically 4 to 6 days.
  • Stucco ceiling removal and modern smoothing in 1960s-80s houses.
  • Drywall preparation and joint taping in new builds.
  • Exterior painting of wood siding, fiber cement and soffits.

Short timelines, real fast service

Buying or selling a home in Saint-Hubert with a tight closing date? We hold short slots every month for exactly that. Same-day estimate by phone and photos for simple projects, contract sent in under 24 hours, work can start within the week.
